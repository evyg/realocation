# TikTok/Reels Video Scripts

## Hook 1: The Shocking Truth (15-30 sec)
**[HOOK]** "I make $150K in San Francisco and I'm basically broke"
**[CONTENT]** Shows calculator: SF rent $3,500, taxes, food... net: $2,100/month
**[PIVOT]** "But if I moved to Austin..." Shows: net $5,200/month
**[CTA]** "Link in bio to find YOUR city"

**[EMOTION]** "That's a down payment. Every. Single. Year."

**[HOOK]** "$150K salary. Let me show you what you ACTUALLY take home in different cities."
**[CONTENT]** 
- NYC: Federal $28K + State $10K + City $5K + FICA $11K = $96K... minus $42K rent = $54K
- Austin: Federal $28K + State $0 + FICA $11K = $111K... minus $24K rent = $87K
**[REVEAL]** "That's $33,000 MORE per year. Same salary."
**[CTA]** "Link calculates this for any salary + any city"

## Production Notes

**Best performing formats:**
1. Green screen + calculator on screen
2. POV/talking head with text overlay
3. Screen recording with voiceover
4. Quick cuts between city comparisons

**Optimal length:** 15-30 seconds for hooks, 45-60 for explainers

**Music suggestions:**
- Trending sounds (check TikTok creative center)
- "Money" by Cardi B (for dramatic reveals)
- Upbeat lo-fi for explainers

**Posting schedule:**
- 3x/day during launch week
- Best times: 7am, 12pm, 7pm local time
- Use all hooks, see what resonates, double down
